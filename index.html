about

Inter IIIT, occurring once every four years, serves as a paramount display of unity and competition among the 23 IIIT colleges nationwide. This unique sports event brings together elite athletes and teams, solidifying its position as a premier competition in the IIIT community. Celebrating a shared passion for sportsmanship and athleticism, Inter IIIT fosters lasting bonds and leaves an enduring mark on intercollegiate sports within the IIIT ecosystem.

Employees' meet

Not just this, Inter IIIT transcends athletic competitions, fostering community through Employee Meets that include Faculty Sports Tournaments. These gatherings unite faculty and staff, emphasizing active participation, teamwork, and friendly competition. This gathering enables faculty and staff from diverse IIITs to engage in collaborative discussions and share insights, creating a parallel camaraderie off the sports field. Like the unity seen in Inter IIIT, the Employee Meet emphasizes shared values and teamwork in shaping the collective success of IIIT institutions.
